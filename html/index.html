ELEGANCE IS AN ATTITUDE

The Premier collection offers refined elegance in dress watches for men. Classic and modern design themes combine in harmony with Seiko’s technology to create a series of stunning timepieces.

Presage combines a Japanese aesthetic sense with traditional craftsmanship and Seiko’s watchmaking skills in an original collection that offers Japanese beauty, quality and long-lasting performance.

SEIKO 5 SPORTS

TECHNOLOGY FOR LIFE

Being famous for reliability, durability and performance that have endeared it to lovers of mechanical watches Seiko 5 Sports after 50 years is re-born. Welcome to the new Seiko 5 Sports, a collection with all the same values but a fresh new look.

SEIKO PROSPEX

TIME FLIES. PROSPEX SOARS.

Seiko Prospex challenges every limit, with a collection of timepieces for sports lovers and adventure seekers whether in the water, in the sky or on land. It offers reliability and safety to both professional and recreational divers.

SEIKO ASTRON

FOR THE LOVE OF WATCHES

The world’s first GPS solar watch. By connecting to the GPS network, the Astron adjusts at the touch of a button to your time zone and, by taking all the energy it needs from light alone, never needs a battery change.
